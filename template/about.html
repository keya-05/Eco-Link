About Us

At Eco Link, we believe that sustainability starts with small actions. Our platform empowers communities to recycle, upcycle, and responsibly dispose of materials through a transparent and fair bidding system.

Founded in 2025 by a group of eco-conscious developers, Eco Link connects individuals and organizations with recyclers, ensuring that waste materials like thermocol, glass, newspapers, batteries, and even old furniture are given a second life.

Our mission is to reduce landfill waste and promote environmental awareness by making recycling accessible and rewarding for everyone. Whether you're a household looking to dispose of recyclables or a business seeking sustainable solutions, Eco Link is your go-to platform.
